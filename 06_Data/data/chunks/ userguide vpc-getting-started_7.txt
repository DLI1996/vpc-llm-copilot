After you've determined the number of VPCs and subnets that you need, what CIDR blocks to assign to your VPCs and subnets, and how to connect your VPC to the internet, you are ready to create your VPC. If you create your VPC using the AWS Management Console and include public subnets in your configuration, we create a route table for the subnet and add the routes required for direct access to the internet.
