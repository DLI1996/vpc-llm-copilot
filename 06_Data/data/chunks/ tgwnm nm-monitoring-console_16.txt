By default, the Topology tree page displays all Sites, Devices, and Customer Gateways of your transit gateway and the logical relationships between them. You can filter the network tree to show specific resources types only to view information about the specific resource it represents. The line colors represent the state of the relationships between AWS and the on-premises resources.
