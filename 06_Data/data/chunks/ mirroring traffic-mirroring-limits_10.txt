The Elastic Network Adapter (ENA) provides checksum offloading capabilities. If a packet is truncated, this might result in the packet checksum not being calculated for the mirrored packet. The following checksums are not calculated when the mirrored packet is truncated:If the mirror packet is truncated, the mirror packet L4 checksum is not calculated.If any part of the L3 header is truncated, the L3 checksum is not calculated.If this causes issues, you can disable ENA checksum offloading on the ENA for the source.
