On the Global networks page, choose the global network ID.In the navigation pane, choose Dashboard.Choose the Topology tree tab. A logical representation of your global network is displayed, along with the details of your global network configuration.(Optional) Filter the information that is displayed in the topology tree by making choices for any combination of the following: Site — Turns the display of sites on or off. Device — Turns the display of devices on or off. Customer gateway — Turns the display of customer gateways on or off.
