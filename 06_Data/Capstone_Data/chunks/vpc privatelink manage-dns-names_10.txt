Check whether the TXT record is publishedYou can verify that your private DNS name domain ownership verification TXT record is published correctly to your DNS server using the following steps. You'll run the nslookup tool, which is available for Windows and Linux.You'll query the DNS servers that serve your domain because those servers contain the most up-to-date information for your domain. Your domain information takes time to propagate to other DNS servers.To verify that your TXT record is published to your DNS serverFind the name servers for your domain using the following command. nslookup -type=NS example.com The output lists the name servers that serve your domain. You'll query one of these servers in the next step.
