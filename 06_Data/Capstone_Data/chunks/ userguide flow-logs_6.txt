After data is captured within an aggregation interval, it takes additional time to process and publish the data to CloudWatch Logs or Amazon S3. The flow log service typically delivers logs to CloudWatch Logs in about 5 minutes and to Amazon S3 in about 10 minutes. However, log delivery is on a best effort basis, and your logs might be delayed beyond the typical delivery time.Default formatWith the default format, the flow log records include the version 2 fields, in the order shown in the available fields table.
