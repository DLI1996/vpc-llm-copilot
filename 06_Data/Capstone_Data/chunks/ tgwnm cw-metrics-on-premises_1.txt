The AWS/NetworkManager namespace includes the following metrics for devices.BytesIn The number of bytes received by the device. BytesOut The number of bytes sent by the device. VpnTunnelsDown The number of VPN tunnels on the device that have a DOWN status. Static VPN tunnels with a DOWN status, and BGP VPN tunnels with any state other than ESTABLISHED, are included in the count.Metric dimensions for devicesTo filter the metrics for your devices, use the following dimensions.DeviceId Filters the metric data by the device.Link metrics
