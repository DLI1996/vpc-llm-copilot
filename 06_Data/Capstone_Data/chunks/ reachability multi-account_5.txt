After you disable trusted access, the users in the management account and delegated administrator accounts can't run a new cross-account analysis. However, they can still see the previously run analyses. Before you can disable trusted access, you must deregister the delegated administrator accounts.You can enable trusted access again after disabling it. However, you must first re-register the delegated administrator accounts.To disable trusted access using the consoleSign in to the management account.Open the Network Manager console athttps://console.aws.amazon.combetworkmanager/home.From the navigation pane, choose Reachability Analyzer, Settings.For Trusted Access, choose Turn off trusted access.
