Open the IAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/iam/.In the navigation pane, choose Policies.Choose Create policy.On the Create policy page, do the following: Choose JSON. Replace the contents of this window with the permissions policy at the start of this section. Choose Next: Tags and Next: Review. Enter a name for your policy and an optional description, and then choose Create policy.In the navigation pane, choose Roles.Choose Create role.
