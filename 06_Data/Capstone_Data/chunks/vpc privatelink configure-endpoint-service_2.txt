Open the Amazon VPC console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/vpc/.In the navigation pane, choose Endpoint services.Select the endpoint service and choose the Allow principals tab.To add permissions, choose Allow principals. For Principals to add, enter the ARN of the principal. To add another principal, choose Add principal. When you are finished adding principals, choose Allow principals.To remove permissions, select the principal and choose Actions, Delete. When prompted for confirmation, enter delete and then choose Delete.To add permissions for your endpoint service using the command linemodify-vpc-endpoint-service-permissions (AWS CLI)Edit-EC2EndpointServicePermission (Tools for Windows PowerShell)Accept or reject connection requests
