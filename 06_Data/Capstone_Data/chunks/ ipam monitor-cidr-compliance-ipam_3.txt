To monitor CIDR usage by resource Open the IPAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/ipam/. In the navigation pane, choose Resources. From the dropdown menu at the top of the content pane, choose the scope that you want to use. For more information about scopes, see How IPAM works. Use the resource CIDR map to view available, allocated, and overlapping IP address space in a scope: Available: An IP address range is available for allocation.
