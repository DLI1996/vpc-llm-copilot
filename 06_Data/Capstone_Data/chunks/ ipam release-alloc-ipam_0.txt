An allocation is a CIDR assignment from an IPAM pool to another resource or IPAM pool.If you are planning to delete a pool, you might need to release a pool allocation. You cannot delete pools if the pools have CIDRs provisioned, and you cannot deprovision CIDRs if the CIDRs are allocated to resources.NoteTo release a manual allocation, use the steps in this section or call the ReleaseIpamPoolAllocation API.
