Deregistering delegated administrators removes that account's permission to manage global networks for your organization. All registered transit gateways from other member accounts are deregistered from the specific delegated administrator's global networks. For more information about how deregistering delegated administrators works, see Deregister delegated administrators.To deregister a delegated administratorAccess the Network Manager console at https://console.aws.amazon.combetworkmanager/home/ with the AWS Organizations management account.Under Connectivity, choose Global Networks.In the navigation pane, choose Settings.
