The Transit gateways page opens, showing a list of your transit gateways.Choose the ID of the transit gateway you want to see more information about.Choose the On-premises associations tab.The Transit Gateway on-premises association page displays the Customer gateway, Device, Link, and State of the transit gateway.To associate a deviceChoose the Customer gateway you want to associate a device with.Choose Associate.On the Edit on-premises association page, choose the Device and optional Link for the association.Choose Edit on-premises association.To disassociate an on-premises deviceChoose the Customer gateway you want to disassociate.Choose Disassociate.Connect peer associationsThe Connect peer associations page displays information about your Connect peers for this transit gateway. You can also disassociate any of your devices.
