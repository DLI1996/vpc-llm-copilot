The custom IAM roles are created automatically through the Network Manager service when you enable multi-account access using the global networks console. We strongly recommend that you use the console for enabling multi-account. Choosing an alternative approach requires an advanced level of expertise, and opens the multi-account for your global network to be more prone to error.CloudWatch-CrossAccountSharingRoleThis policy provides delegated administrators and the management accounts access to CloudWatch monitoring data from other member accounts.
