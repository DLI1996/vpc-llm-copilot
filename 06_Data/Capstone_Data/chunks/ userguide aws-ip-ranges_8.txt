The subset of IP address ranges. The addresses listed for API_GATEWAY are egress only. Specify AMAZON to get all IP address ranges (meaning that every subset is also in the AMAZON subset). However, some IP address ranges are only in the AMAZON subset (meaning that they are not also available in another subset).
