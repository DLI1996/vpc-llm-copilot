Add rules for inbound traffic under Inbound rules as follows: Choose Add rule. Choose HTTP for Type and enter the IP address range of your private subnet for Source. Choose Add rule. Choose HTTPS for Type and enter the IP address range of your private subnet for Source. Choose Add rule. Choose SSH for Type and enter the IP address range of your network for Source.Add rules for outbound traffic under Outbound rules as follows: Choose Add rule.
