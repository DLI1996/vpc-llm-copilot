Managed resource: A managed resource has a CIDR allocated from an IPAM pool. IPAM monitors the CIDR for potential IP address overlap with other CIDRs in the pool, and monitors the CIDR’s compliance with a pool’s allocation rules. IPAM supports managing the following type of resources: VPCs Public IPv4 pools ImportantPublic IPv4 pools and IPAM pools are managed by distinct resources in AWS. Public IPv4 pools are single account resources that enable you to convert your publicly-owned CIDRs to Elastic IP addresses. IPAM pools can be used to allocate your public space to public IPv4 pools.
