When you onboard to CloudWatch Logs Insights, the following occurs: A CloudWatch Events rule with the name DON_NOT_DELETE_networkmanager_rule is created in the US West (Oregon) Region. A CloudWatch Logs group with the name /aws/eventsbetworkmanagerloggroup is created in the US West (Oregon) Region. A CloudWatch Events rule is configured with the CloudWatch Logs group as a target.
