After you create your global network, you can monitor your networks through this dashboard. You can view network activity and health using Amazon CloudWatch metrics and Amazon CloudWatch Events. The global networks dashboard can help you identify whether issues in your network are caused by AWS resources, your on-premises resources, or the connections between them.global networks does not create, modify, or delete your transit gateways and their attachments.
