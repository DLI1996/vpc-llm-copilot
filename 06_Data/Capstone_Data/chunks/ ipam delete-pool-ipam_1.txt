To delete a pool Open the IPAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/ipam/. In the navigation pane, choose Pools. From the dropdown menu at the top of the content pane, choose the scope that you want to use. For more information about scopes, see How IPAM works. In the content pane, choose the pool whose CIDR you want to delete. Choose Actions > Delete pool. Enter delete and then choose Delete.
