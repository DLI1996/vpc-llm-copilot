AWSDocumentationAmazon VPCIP Address ManagerManually allocate a CIDR to a pool to reserve IP address spaceFollow the steps in this section to manually allocate a CIDR to a pool. You might do this in order to reserve a CIDR within an IPAM pool for later use. You can also reserve space in your IPAM pool to represent an on-premises network. IPAM will manage that reservation for you and indicate if any CIDRs overlap with your on-premises IP space.
