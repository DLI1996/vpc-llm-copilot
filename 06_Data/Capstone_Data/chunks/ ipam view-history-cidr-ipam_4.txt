To view the history of a CIDR Open the IPAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/ipam/. In the navigation pane, choose Search IP history. Enter an IPv4 or IPv6 IP address or CIDR. This must be a specific CIDR for the resource. Choose an IPAM scope ID. Choose a date/time range. If you want to filter the results by VPC, enter a VPC ID. Use this option if the CIDR appears in multiple VPCs. Choose Search.
