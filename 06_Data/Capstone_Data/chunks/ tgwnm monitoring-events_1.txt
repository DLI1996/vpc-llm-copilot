Before you can view events for your global network, you must onboard to CloudWatch Logs Insights. In the global networks console, choose the ID of your global network. In the Network events summary section, choose Onboard to CloudWatch Log Insights.An IAM principal in your account, such as an IAM user, must have sufficient permissions to onboard to CloudWatch Logs Insights.
