For Amazon side Autonomous System Number (ASN), enter the private ASN for your transit gateway. This should be the ASN for the AWS side of a Border Gateway Protocol (BGP) session. The range is from 64512 to 65534 for 16-bit ASNs. The range is from 4200000000 to 4294967294 for 32-bit ASNs.
