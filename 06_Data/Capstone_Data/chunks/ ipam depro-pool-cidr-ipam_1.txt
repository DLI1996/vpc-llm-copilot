To deprovision a pool CIDR Open the IPAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/ipam/. In the navigation pane, choose Pools. From the dropdown menu at the top of the content pane, choose the scope that you want to use. For more information about scopes, see How IPAM works. In the content pane, choose the pool whose CIDRs you want to deprovision. Choose the CIDRs tab. Select one or more CIDRs and choose Deprovision CIDRs. Choose Deprovision CIDR.
