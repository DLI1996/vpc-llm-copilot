Step 1: Associate an IPv6 CIDR block with your VPC and subnets Associate an Amazon-provided or BYOIP IPv6 CIDR block with your VPC and with your subnets. Step 2: Update your route tables Update your route tables to route your IPv6 traffic. For a public subnet, create a route that routes all IPv6 traffic from the subnet to the internet gateway. For a private subnet, create a route that routes all internet-bound IPv6 traffic from the subnet to an egress-only internet gateway.
