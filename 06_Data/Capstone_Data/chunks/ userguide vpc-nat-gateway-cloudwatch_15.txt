Choose the NATGateway metric namespace and then choose a metric dimension. When you get to the metrics, select the check box next to the BytesOutToDestination metric for the NAT gateway, and then choose Select metric.Configure the alarm as follows, and then choose Next: For Statistic, choose Sum. For Period, choose 15 minutes. For Whenever, choose Greater/Equal and enter 5000000 for the threshold.For Notification, select an existing SNS topic or choose Create new topic to create a new one. Choose Next.Enter a name and description for the alarm and choose Next.
