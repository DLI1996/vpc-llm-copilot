Choose Switch Role. You're logged out of your current account and into that member account. A new tab opens showing the details of the resource. For example, if you choose a VPC resource, the VPC resource page opens for the member account that owns the resource.Depending on the delegated permission level assigned to the delegated administrators and the management account when trusted access was enabled, you can either view information (read-only permission) about the resource or add/modify (administrator permission) the resource.
