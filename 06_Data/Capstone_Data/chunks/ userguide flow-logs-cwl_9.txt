For Filter, specify the type of traffic to log. Choose All to log accepted and rejected traffic, Reject to log only rejected traffic, or Accept to log only accepted traffic.For Maximum aggregation interval, choose the maximum period of time during which a flow is captured and aggregated into one flow log record.For Destination, choose Send to CloudWatch Logs.For Destination log group, choose the name of an existing log group or enter the name of a new log group that will be created when you create this flow log.For IAM role, specify the name of the role that has permissions to publish logs to CloudWatch Logs.
