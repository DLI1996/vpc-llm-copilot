These locations are composed of Regions, Availability Zones, Local Zones, and Wavelength Zones. Each Region is a separate geographic area.Availability Zones are multiple, isolated locations within each Region.Local Zones allow you to place resources, such as compute and storage, in multiple locations closer to your end users.AWS Outposts brings native AWS services, infrastructure, and operating models to virtually any data center, co-location space, or on-premises facility.
