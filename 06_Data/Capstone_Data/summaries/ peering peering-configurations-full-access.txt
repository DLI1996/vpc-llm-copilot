This document provides information about VPC peering configurations in AWS. It explains how to configure VPC peering connections so that route tables have access to the entire CIDR block of the peer VPC. It also provides examples of different VPC peering configurations, including two VPCs peered together, one VPC peered with two VPCs, three VPCs peered together, and multiple VPCs peered together. The document includes instructions on how to update route tables for each configuration.
